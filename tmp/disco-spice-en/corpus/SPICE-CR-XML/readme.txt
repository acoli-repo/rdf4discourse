Disco-SPICE (Spoken conversations from the SPICE-Ireland corpus annotated with discourse relations)

The xml-files in this folder contain annotations according to the PDTB 3.0 and CCR frameworks for telephone and broadcast interview conversations from the SPICE corpus.
You can view these annotations by downloading EXMERaLDA (http://www.exmaralda.org/en/downloads/), and opening PartiturEditor. The xml-files can then be opened.

Reference: Rehbein, I., Scholman, M.C.J., Demberg, V. (2016). Annotating discourse relations in spoken language: A comparison of the PDTB and CCR frameworks. Proceedings of the 10th International Conference on Language Resources and Evaluation (LREC 16), Portoro≈æ, Slovenia.
